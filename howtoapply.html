Description
1. After visiting the website the applicant needs to Login/Sign up in the webbsite
2. In order to create a account the applicant should be above 18 years of age and the applicant must have a Aadhar card in order to register and apply.
3. After successfully signing in/Log in, the applicant will have to fill the application form. The applicant must keep aadhar card with him while filling the appolication form.
4. After correctly filling the application form the applicant should print the application form before submitting it.
5. After successfully submitting the application form the applicant will get the appointment date. The applicant will have to visit the RTO Office on the alloted appointment date between 10.00 am to 4.00 pm.
6. The applicant must note that he must carry the original valid address proof(Society maintainence bill, Electricity bill, Gas Bill, Telephone Bill, Rationing Card) along with the photocopy.
7. The applicant must note that he must carry the original valid photo id (Aadhar Card, Pan Card, Voter Id)along with the photocopy.
8. The applicant must carry 4 passport size color photographs while visiting the RTO Office.
9. If the applicant fails to produce any of the document or is not able to visit the RTO office on the appointment date then the applicant will have to freshly take the appointment again.
